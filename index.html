The topic of this page is "How Music Affects Anxiety"

Music is a big part of many people's lives. Everywhere we go, whatever we do, there will always be music playing either through our headphones or through the speakers in the distance. In this data set, we can see how much of life music really affects. Specifically, we can see how our anxiety levels are affected by music. Anxiety is also a big part of our lives. It controls more aspects of it than we realize. And through this data set, we can analyze whether these concepts have a relation or not.
